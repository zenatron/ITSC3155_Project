1 Month: Self-Assessment and Goal Setting

Identify Career Goals: Reflect on your interests, skills, and career aspirations. Consider industries and roles that align with your strengths and passions.
Skill Gap Analysis: Compare your current skills with those required for your targeted job roles. Identify any gaps that need to be filled.
Create a Plan: Set specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goals for acquiring skills, gaining experience, and applying for jobs.
